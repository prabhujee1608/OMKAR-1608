ABES Engineering College, established in 2000, is a private AKTU-affiliated engineering and management institute. With NAAC grade A and NBA accreditation, this college has been ranked in the 201st position among other engineering colleges in India by NIRF in 2024. ABES Engineering College offers UG and PG programs like B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and MBA. B.Tech, being the flagship course, is available across CSE, AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, ECE, Electrical & Computer Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Both direct and entrance-based admissions are conducted. But admission to B.Tech programs is based on performance in the JEE Main. Seats are allocated based on UPTAC counselling rounds. The average fee for B.Tech 1st year is INR 1.34 Lakhs. ABES Engineering College placement records for 2025 are yet to be released. But according to the latest 2024 placement statistics, the highest package secured was INR 59.91 LPA by B.Tech departments. A total of 1683 students have been placed across all the UG & PG departments. 560+ companies visited the placement drive. It includes some of the top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Goldman Sachs, Walmart, TCS, and Accenture.
